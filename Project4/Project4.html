CARDIACS

Shunned by music critics in their time, the english band Cardiacs are the most talented and unique band that you have never heard of.

With only 5 proper albums released between 1988 and 1999 the short lived band sounded like no-one else before or since.

Multiple time signature and key changes within a single song, high energy and virtuoso musicianship define the sound of Cardiacs. They effortlessly blended the energy and ethos of punk with the maestro level musicianship of prog.



  • Albums released;
    1. A Little Man and a House and the Whole World Windows (1988)
    2. On Land and in the Sea (1989)
    3. Heaven Born and Ever Bright (1992)
    4. Sing to GOD (1996)
    5. Guns (1999)
    6. LSD (Released posthumously in 2025, completed by others)
